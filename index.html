Hi, I'm Dawn. UIUX Designer.

I currently work at BANF, a smart tire sensor company, where I lead the design of our Fleet Management System, BANFLEET. My main focus is turning massive amounts of tire sensor data into clear, easy-to-understand visuals for everyone.

Alongside this, I led the design for a consumer tire platform called GooseTire. I worked on everything from early planning and branding to creating a simple, one-stop buying process. I also built the admin dashboard to help run the business smoothly.

Before that, I designed the UX/UI for 'Meta-Fab' at Zinnotech. It is a teamwork tool for highly secure places like semiconductor factories. I designed a remote experience that helps outside experts and inside workers communicate safely and naturally, just as if they were in the same room.

To me, design is much more than just a job. I truly love the process of taking cold, complex technologies and shaping them into highly polished products that naturally fit into people's everyday lives.
